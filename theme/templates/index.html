what we do

Legion GIS, LLC is a small consulting firm specializing in Geographic Information Systems. Though a young company, we have already worked on a wide variety of geospatial applications with clients from different backgrounds. We excel in working closely with our clients and partners, and relish new challenges. We have produced professional cartographic products, created ArcGIS toolboxes, designed spatial databases, and led GIS-focused outreach and education efforts. We also offer webmap hosting, and are one of few companies offering full development and hosting services for the Arches inventory platform.
